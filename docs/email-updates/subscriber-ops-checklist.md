# Subscriber Operations Checklist

> Scaffold only. No real email should be sent from this repository.

Use this checklist before any future CVCalculator rate or article update email.

## Before Drafting

- [ ] Confirm the source rate update from an authoritative source.
- [ ] Record the source URL, publication date, and retrieval date.
- [ ] Confirm whether the change is meaningful enough to justify an email.
- [ ] Confirm the update is educational, not promotional-only.

## Website First

- [ ] Update the relevant CVCalculator.ca article before preparing the email.
- [ ] Verify the article is accurate, cautious, and not personalized advice.
- [ ] Verify the article is live on the production website.
- [ ] Confirm the live URL works with a cache-busting query string if needed.

## Email Preparation

- [ ] Prepare the email using `rate-update-template.md`.
- [ ] Keep the message concise and educational.
- [ ] State clearly that rates are only one input into commuted value estimates.
- [ ] Avoid language implying guaranteed outcomes.
- [ ] Avoid personalized financial, tax, legal, actuarial, or investment advice.
- [ ] Keep the app CTA secondary to the educational explanation.
- [ ] Link to relevant CVCalculator.ca articles.

## Compliance and Delivery Gate

- [ ] Confirm subscriber consent records exist.
- [ ] Confirm unsubscribe handling is available and tested.
- [ ] Confirm privacy language and sender identity are appropriate.
- [ ] Confirm delivery mechanics are approved.
- [ ] Confirm no secrets or provider credentials are stored in this repository.
- [ ] Confirm the approved email mechanism exists.

No email should be sent until consent, unsubscribe, privacy, sender identity, and delivery mechanics are confirmed.

## Final Review

- [ ] Review the subject line and preheader for accuracy.
- [ ] Review all links.
- [ ] Review the disclaimer.
- [ ] Review for no personalized advice.
- [ ] Confirm the rate values match the authoritative source.
- [ ] Confirm the email does not imply market timing or investment advice.

## Sending

- [ ] Send only when an approved email mechanism exists.
- [ ] Send only to the intended consented audience.
- [ ] Do not manually copy subscriber lists into unapproved tools.

## Post-Send Log

Record:

- Send date
- Audience
- Subject
- Preheader
- Linked article URL
- Source rate update URL
- Sender/reviewer
- Notes or issues
