## Why It Matters

Commuted values depend on multiple factors, including pension amount, age, earliest unreduced retirement date, plan rules, mortality assumptions, and CIA Section 3500 interest rates.

Interest rates are only one input. In general, higher discount rates can reduce estimated commuted values, while lower discount rates can increase them. The actual effect for any member depends on their own pension details and plan assumptions.
