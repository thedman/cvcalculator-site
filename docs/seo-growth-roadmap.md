# CVCalculator SEO Growth Roadmap

## Purpose

This document is the primary SEO growth planning record for CVCalculator.ca.

It captures strategic decisions, measurement baselines, quality gates, and review criteria for organic acquisition work. Production implementation details remain in the root HTML files, `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt`, `README.md`, `CLAUDE.md`, and `AGENTS.md`.

## SEO Growth Round 1 - Closed

### Objective

Establish topical authority for Canadian defined benefit pension decisions.

### Outcome

Achieved.

### Evidence

- Homepage is no longer the only organic acquisition surface.
- LIRA, OMERS, CIA rates, and calculator-support pages generate meaningful organic traffic.
- HOOPP and OTPP demonstrate early discovery signals.
- Internal linking has formed a coherent pension education cluster.
- Organic subscribers validate trust beyond search clicks.

### Baseline Metrics

Source: Google Search Console export `cvcalculator.ca-Performance-on-Search-2026-07-07.xlsx`, covering June 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026.

These metrics are the comparison baseline for SEO Growth Round 2.

### Lessons Learned

- Topical clusters outperform isolated articles.
- Evergreen educational content compounds.
- High editorial quality is a competitive advantage.
- Internal linking strengthens topical authority.
- Search Console should guide expansion priorities.
- Editorial QA reduces rework and improves consistency.
- Mobile experience appears to be a significant strength and should be preserved.

### Decision

Proceed to SEO Growth Round 2.

Round 1 objectives have been met. Future investment should prioritize expanding the pension-plan cluster while preserving the established editorial quality standard.

Success will be measured through distributed acquisition across multiple qualified landing pages rather than homepage growth alone.

## SEO Growth Round 2 - Pension Plan Cluster Expansion

### Objective

Demonstrate that the pension-plan guide model scales without reducing editorial quality.

### Primary KPI

Homepage click share decreases while total organic clicks increase.

This KPI must be interpreted as a pair. A falling homepage share is positive only when total organic clicks are stable or growing and non-homepage pages are gaining traffic.

### Secondary KPIs

- Non-homepage clicks increase.
- Additional pension-plan guides become meaningful landing pages.
- Existing evergreen content maintains rankings.
- Mobile CTR remains strong.
- App CTA engagement from article pages grows.

### Additional KPI

Track unique landing pages with at least 10 organic clicks per month.

Current baseline:

- Homepage
- LIRA
- OMERS
- CIA rates
- Calculator Canada

Success comes from increasing meaningful contributors, not merely publishing additional URLs.

### Readiness Gate

A new pension-plan guide should begin only when:

- Search Console demonstrates adjacent query demand.
- The pension plan has a genuine plan-specific differentiator.
- Existing pages have had time to stabilize.
- The article can meet the established editorial QA standard.
- The guide strengthens the overall topical cluster.

Do not publish plan guides on a fixed cadence if the evidence or quality gate is not met.

## Quarterly Review

Evaluate:

- Homepage click share.
- Total clicks.
- Unique landing pages with at least 10 clicks per month.
- Top-performing pension-plan pages.
- Search Console query expansion.
- Mobile vs. desktop trends.
- Subscriber growth.
- App acquisition signals.

Adjust Round 2 priorities using evidence rather than publication cadence.
